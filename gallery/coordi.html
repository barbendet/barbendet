As my Capstone project for Parsons, I worked to develop a wooden educational toy that allows kids ages 3+ to work on their fine motor skills (specifically matching, threading, and balancing).

What makes this toy special, is the play pattern being open ended, while also heavily focusing on fine motor skills. It does not force the kids to play in any certain way, yet still helps them improve.
